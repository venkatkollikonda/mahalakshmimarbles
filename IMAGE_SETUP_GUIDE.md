## ğŸ“¸ **DIY PHOTOGRAPHY TIPS**

### **Taking Good Product Photos:**

1. **Lighting**:
   - Natural daylight is best
   - Avoid direct harsh sunlight
   - Overcast day = perfect lighting

2. **Background**:
   - Clean, neutral background
   - White or grey works well
   - Remove clutter

3. **Angle**:
   - Straight overhead shot
   - 45-degree angle for depth
   - Close-up for texture details

4. **Consistency**:
   - Same distance from camera
   - Same lighting conditions
   - Same background for all

5. **Details**:
   - Show the actual pattern/texture
   - Capture the shine/finish
   - Multiple shots per product
