Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What types of stones do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of granites and marbles sourced from India and around the world, including premium varieties for countertops, flooring, and wall cladding.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services by our experienced team of craftsmen. Installation is included in most of our packages.

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies depending on the project size, but typically ranges from 1-3 days for residential projects.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, we offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and installation workmanship to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Can I visit your showroom?

Absolutely! We welcome you to visit our showroom during business hours to see our full collection and speak with our design consultants.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we offer free consultations and quotes. Contact us with your project details and we'll provide a detailed estimate.
