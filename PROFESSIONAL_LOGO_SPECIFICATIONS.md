## ‚úÖ DESIGN PRINCIPLES TO FOLLOW

### 1. Simplicity
- Clean, not cluttered
- 3 colors maximum
- Easily recognizable at small sizes

### 2. Scalability
- Must work at 16px and 4000px
- Details should remain visible when small
- Test at multiple sizes

### 3. Versatility
- Works on light and dark backgrounds
- Black & white version looks good
- Works in single color (gold only)

### 4. Timelessness
- Avoid trendy effects
- Classic proportions
- No gradients that date quickly
- Should look good in 10+ years

### 5. Appropriateness
- Must look like granite/marble business
- Professional, not playful
- Premium, not cheap
- Traditional meets modern

### 6. Memorability
- Unique, not generic
- One strong visual element
- Easy to describe ("the one with...")

---

## üö´ DESIGN DON'TS

### Avoid These Elements:
‚ùå Generic geometric shapes (just circles/triangles)
‚ùå Overly complex illustrations
‚ùå Stock images or clipart
‚ùå Too many colors (> 3)
‚ùå Thin fonts that disappear at small sizes
‚ùå 3D effects that don't translate to print
‚ùå Photographic textures (use vector patterns)
‚ùå Comic/playful fonts
‚ùå Lightning bolts, stars, badges (overused)

### Common Mistakes:
‚ùå Logo too detailed for small sizes
‚ùå Text too thin/light
‚ùå Colors that don't print well
‚ùå Files not provided in all formats
‚ùå No clear space guidelines
‚ùå Can't work in single color

## üìè LAYOUT & SPACING

### Safe Zone (Clear Space)
- Minimum clear space: Height of "M" in company name
- No other elements within this zone
- Applies to all sides

### Alignment
- Center-aligned for square/badge version
- Left-aligned for horizontal version
- Icon and text properly balanced

### Proportions
- Icon/Symbol: 30-40% of total height
- Company Name: 50-60% of total height
- Tagline: 10-15% of total height
